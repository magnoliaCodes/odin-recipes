Description

This Lasagna Recipe is easy to make with a creamy ricotta cheese mixture, savory meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese. BONUS: it’s make-ahead and freezer-friendly!

Ingredients

Cheese Filling

  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese, 2 cups
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • ¾ cup Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ tsp pepper

Meat Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, finely diced
  • ¾ lb. ground beef
  • ¾ lb. ground Italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz. marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Lasagna Noodles / Cheese Topping

  • 12 lasagna noodles, plus extra in case of breakage
  • 2.5 cups mozzarella cheese

Instructions

Prep Work

  1. Combine the cheese filling ingredients in a medium bowl and set aside. Measure out remaining ingredients.

Make the Meat Sauce

  1. Heat olive oil over medium heat and add the diced onions. Let them soften for 10 minutes.
  2. Add the ground beef and sausage and increase heat to medium-high. Break up the meat with a spatula and cook until browned, about 8–10 minutes. Add garlic in the last minute. Drain excess grease.
  3. Add chicken broth and scrape the bottom of the pot. Add marinara sauce, tomato paste, hot sauce, and Worcestershire. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Cover partially and let simmer while you cook the noodles.

Preheat Oven and Cook the Lasagna Noodles

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil salted water and cook noodles to al dente. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking. Drain and rinse with cold water. Lay noodles flat on wax or parchment paper.

Assemble

  1. Spread 1 heaping cup of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9×13-inch casserole dish. Layer 4 noodles over the sauce.
  2. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture over noodles, followed by 1½ cups of meat sauce.
  3. Repeat layers two more times, ending with meat sauce on top.
  4. Top with 2½ cups of mozzarella cheese.
  5. Cover with foil sprayed with nonstick spray (spray side down to prevent sticking).

Bake

  1. Bake for 35 minutes. Remove foil and bake for 10 more minutes until cheese is bubbly.
  2. Remove from oven and let sit for 15 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh parsley.
